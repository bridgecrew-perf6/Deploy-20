Teeth Whitening

What is a teeth withening?

Teeth whitening is the most common cosmetic dental treatment, performed either at home or in dental practice.
There is a wide variety of teeth whitening products readily available at the stores, however with inconsistent
success rates. Depending on the precision of application and the overall dental health, the results vary from
person to person and due to a low concentration of the whitening ingredient (hydrogen peroxide) may take months
to achieve visible results.

Why should you consider a teeth whitening?

Professional teeth whitening, offers the safest and quickest way to achieve bright smile. Dental professional
ill first remove any plaque or tartar buildup that would prevent the whitening product penetrating your teeth.
Furthermore, whitening products may cause gum sensitivity, therefore it is vital to assess what strength of whitening
agent would ensure that the whitening process will no cause any irritation. However, the greatest benefit of teeth whitening
with professional dental products is the effectiveness in removing tough stains and the ability to achieve astonishing results
even after first application.
