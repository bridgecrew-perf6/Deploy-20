Porcelain Veneers

What are porcelain veneers?

Veneers are thin custom-made ceramic shells that are attached to the outer layer of natural teeth.
They are used to improve the appearance of chipped, worn, discolored, uneven or irregularly shaped
teeth. They also offer an alternative to orthodontic treatments, by filling the gaps between unevenly
spaced teeth or optically correcting their alignment.

When should you consider veneers?

Veneers can be used to cover a single damaged tooth, or cover all teeth for esthetic reasons,
to create a bright and beautiful smile. Regardless of the reason for choosing porcelain veneers,
very little preparation has to be completed before a veneer can be fitted, as an only small portion
of enamel is removed to make space for the veneer to attach. Veneers are glued to your teeth one
by one, using composite resin cement, creating a permanent bond. Shortly after, you are ready to
enjoy your beautiful smile.
