What is Ultrasonic Teeth Cleaning?

A dental bridge is an excellent restorative solution to missing one or more teeth, that will restores your bite and Regular cleaning and flossing are not enough to maintain a healthy smile.
We recommend that you attend regular hygiene cleanings that combined
with dental examinations will ensure that any buildup around your teeth
and at gum line is removed before it can cause tooth decay.

What is the dental cleaning procedure?

The teeth cleaning procedure is completed by one of our highly trained dental hygienists. Initially,
the dental hygienist will conduct an oral examination and assess the general health of your oral
tissue after the professional teeth cleaning will begin. The dental hygienist will use either manual or
ultrasonic teeth cleaning tools. While both are very effective in plaque and tartar removal, the ultrasonic
scaler is able to penetrate deeper into periodontal pockets than the manual instrument. It will also be kinder
to the teeth structure, as the ultrasonic scaler uses vibrations at a high frequency to break up any deposit.
It also has a smaller tip, therefore causes less tissue distention and requires less pressure, making the professional
cleaning experience less time consuming and more comfortable for the patient.
