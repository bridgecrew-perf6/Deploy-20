Ottawa's Downtown Dentistry

UOttawa Dental Centre has been providing the student community with high-quality, gentle dental care since 1993.
We pride ourselves in offering you and your family a complete range of dental services in one convenient location.
The goal of our knowledgeable and experienced team is to see that you receive an exceptional dental experience at every visit.
We are committed to your safety and comfort.

A beautiful smile starts with a healthy mouth. A great dental experience starts at UOttawa Dentist.
Our dental team is dedicated to providing you with the exceptional service and quality dental care you deserve.
Our patients benefit from our comprehensive dental services, modern technology, and appreciate our patient-centered
approach. We are your partner in optimal oral health, and together, we'll achieve your smile goals.
Our dedicated dental team provides patients with a full array of services including general and preventive dentistry,
cosmetic dentistry, sedation dentistry, restorative dentistry, as well as neuromuscular and periodontal care.
And because every smile is different, we provide individualized treatment plans that address your concerns,
from esthetic services such as teeth whitening and porcelain veneers, to restorative therapies that revitalize such as
CEREC crowns and dental implants. We offer soothing oral, IV, Nitrous Oxide sedation so that your visit with us is as comfortable
and relaxing as possible.
A Healthy Smile Contributes to a Healthy Life!

We are committed to providing the highest quality of clinical care and personalized service in a
warm and compassionate atmosphere. Our doctors and staff enjoy getting to know each patient and their families,
and love creating relationships that last multiple generations.
