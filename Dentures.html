Dentures

What are dentures?

Dentures are artificial removable appliances that can replace lost or removed natural teeth.
They are custom designed to fit your mouth and they can either replace all teeth (full denture) or
just a single tooth (partial denture). Regardless of the reason for tooth loss, dentures go far beyond restoring
the appearance of your smile.

Why should you consider a dental bridge?

With the loss of teeth, you are unable to eat foods that require chewing, forcing you to change your diet.
With dentures you are able to keep your diet the same, ensuring that your body received all the nutrients it requires.
Furthermore, missing teeth can have a negative impact on any remaining teeth, supporting structures around the cheeks,
lips and speech. When you lose your teeth, facial muscles can sag, making you look older. Dentures can help fill out the
appearance of your face and profile. They can also prevent shifting of existing teeth and protect the gums, decreasing the
risk of developing further issues such as serious bite problems, bleeding gums and jaw pains.
